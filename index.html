Features

AI and CV-Enabled Documentation

Physiotherapy is non-verbal. AccuDocs captures what other tools miss.

Computer Vision
Sees the clinical picture

The computer vision model tracks joints in real-time, analysing subtle movement patterns, calculating angles, rep counts and asymmetry — objective data that verbal tools simply cannot capture.

  • Exercise type identification
  • Repetition & set counting
  • Assistance level detection (min/mod/max)
  • Joint angle & ROM measurement
  • Asymmetry & gait pattern analysis
Voice Intelligence
Hears the clinical story

The hands-free microphone captures your conversation — therapist cues, patient responses, pain levels — and maps them to the right SOAPIER sections automatically.

  • Medical terminology recognition
  • Patient response capture
  • Pain scale documentation
